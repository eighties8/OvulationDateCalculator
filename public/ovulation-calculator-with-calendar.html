Understanding Your Fertility Calendar

When using our ovulation calculator with calendar, you'll see different types of days marked:

  • Menstrual Days: The days of your period (typically 3-7 days)
  • Fertile Window: The 5-6 days leading up to ovulation plus ovulation day
  • Ovulation Day: The specific day when ovulation occurs
  • Peak Fertility: The 2-3 days around ovulation with highest conception chances
  • Regular Days: Non-fertile days of your cycle
